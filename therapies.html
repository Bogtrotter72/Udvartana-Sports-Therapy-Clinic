Massage Therapies
Holistic Massage

Massage has been practised for centuries. It can be used as a therapeutic, healing treatment, as well as for revitalizing, repairing and relaxing the body.

Modern massage techniques have evolved mainly from a system developed by the Swedish physiologist, Per Henrik Ling.

Holistic massage therapies are designed to help you relax both your mind and body, and are based on the classical Swedish massage.

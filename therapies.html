Massage Therapies
Holistic Massage

Massage has been practised for centuries. It can be used as a therapeutic, healing treatment, as well as for revitalizing, repairing and relaxing the body. Modern massage techniques have evolved mainly from a system that is often inaccurately credited to the work of Swedish physiologist, Per Henrik Ling. It was, in fact, the Dutch practitioner Johann Georg Mezger who first applied the French terms that are the names for the basic strokes.

These massage techniques vary from light to vigorous and generally focusses around five styles of strokes. The five basic strokes are effleurage (sliding or gliding), petrissage (kneading), tapotement (rhythmic tapping), friction (cross fiber or with the fibers) and vibration/shaking. The term "Swedish" massage is actually only recognized in English and Dutch speaking countries, and in Hungary. Elsewhere the style is referred to as "classic massage". Clinical studies have found that massage can reduce chronic pain, fatigue, joint stiffness, and improve function in patients with osteoarthritis of the knee.

The term Holistic refers to the whole person, mind, body, and spirit. Sometimes the tensions in our body is a reflection of mental and emotional stress and vice versa. Our Holistic massage treatments aid in removing toxins from your body,and are designed to bring about a deep state of relaxation and reduce muscle tension and stress. This will encourage your body's natural ability to heal and help restore balance to your entire body as a whole.

Deep-Tissue Massage

Deep tissues massage is a classical massage technique that focusses on deeper layers of muscle. The movements are slower and involve firmer pressure than those used in holistic massage.

Where the aim of holistic massage was to bring about a deep state of relaxation, the main aim of deep tissue massage is to treat areas of chronic muscle tension and injury. Other goals include the alleviation of cramp, treatment of trigger points, improving range of motion and improving musculosketal imbalances and postural problems.

Due to the firm pressures applied during deep tissue massage, it is recommended that you consulate with your doctor if you have any underlying medical conditions. You should also feel free to discuss any concerns you might have with one of our therapists.

Sports Massage

Sports massage is a specific form of massage that is used to help maintain the health of athletes and enhance their performance; amateur and professional alike.

Generally speaking, sports massage techniques may help you improve your mobility and range of movement, as well as helping your prepare for, and recover from, strenuous training sessions. When it comes to the event, our therapists can help you prepare physically and mentally for the challenges of the event, help you recover during event stages and also help you recover post-event. Should you injure yourself during the event, our highly trained sports therapists will support you in your recovery.

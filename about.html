Udvartana Sports Therapy Clinic

Revitalize, Repair, Relax

Udvartana Sports Therapy Clinic is dedicated to the holistic well-being of all its clients.

Udvartana is a word for massage that is founded in an ancient tradition of healing through touch. It is our belief that our minds and bodies are inextricably linked and that if one suffers, so does the other. Many of us are aware that when we are stressed or worried, our body can suffer - headaches, back pain, upset stomach; there are a number of ways in which our bodies reflect our troubles. Similarly, if our body is tense, or in pain our minds and thoughts can become troubled.

At Udvartana we offer a range of treatments that are proven to help you...
Revitalize, Repair and Relax.

Asta

Asta is the chief therapist at the clinic, she has a background in sports education, and has been practising sports therapy massage for a number of years. She also has a sporting background, competing as an amateur in field events for Lithuania and specializing in 100m hurdles.

Asta holds additional certification in anti-cellulite and detox therapy treatments.

Speaking four languages fluently, she is able to offer consultations to native Russian, Polish, Lithuanian and English speakers.

Lina

Lina has recently qualified as a level 3 therapist and, with that, brings the latest developments in sports massage therapy to the clinic.

Lina enjoys adventure sports and has participated in a wide variety of activities from mountain climbing to canoeing and has even run the London marathon.

Lina is currently studying Indian head massage and reflexology therapies and will soon be able to offer clients a range of treatments, from head to toe.
